Programs

Seminar

Yeagerist highschool will invite some people from many job, like police, bartender, CEO, painter, etc. to give our student many perspective of life. We hope with our seminar can make our student find their interest and boost their motivation to improve their talent.

Retret

Student in 3rd year will stay overnight for 3 day 2 night in our chosen place. in 3 days and 2 night the student will provide with spiritual lesson to strenghten their mental. with retret we hope student can grow stronger to face their own problem.

Outbound

This program will have the first year student to stay 2 day 1 night for leadership training. this program will help student to polish their leadership skill, not only that this program also improve their teamwork ability.

Camping

This program will have the student to stay 2 day 1 night to camp in nature. this program will grow our student care for nature so we can keep our nature in check. we hope with this program the student can care for the nature.

Live In

Student in 2nd year will have to stay in small village for a week. this program will improve their humanity to help people with low economic. we hope that this program can grow humanity out from every and each student.

Study Tour

Each year the student will travel to historic place or company to improve their knowledge. like many said knowledge is power.
